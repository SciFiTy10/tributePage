Here's a time line of Jeff Bezos's life:
  • 1964 - Jeff Bezos was born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, to a teenage mother, Jacklyn Gise Jorgensen.
  • 1986 - Bezos pursued his interest in computers at Princeton University, where he graduated summa cum laude in 1986 with a degree in computer science and electrical engineering.
  • 1994 - He quit his job in 1994, moved to Seattle and targeted the untapped potential of the internet market by opening an online bookstore.
  • 1995 - After inviting 300 friends to beta test the site, Bezos opened Amazon.com, named after the meandering South American River, on July 16, 1995.
  • 1997 - Amazon.com went public in 1997, leading many market analysts to question whether the company could hold its own when traditional retailers launched their own e-commerce sites.
